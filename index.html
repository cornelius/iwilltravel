My story of Stanford's HCI class 2012

Earlier this year I participated in Stanford's Human-Computer Interaction class. Part of the class was a practical project. The goal was to live the process of creating human-computer-interaction with some real use case and application, and to things like finding user's needs, gathering feedback on quick prototypes, iterating, and testing different solutions. This is the story of my project, which ended up being a personal travel assistant.
